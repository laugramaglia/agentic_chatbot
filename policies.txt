Return Policy:
- We accept returns within 30 days of purchase.
- To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
- It must also be in the original packaging.
- To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase.

Shipping Policy:
- We ship to all locations within the United States.
- Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-2 business days.
- Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days.
- Expedited shipping is available for an additional fee.

Payment Methods:
- We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express).
- We also accept PayPal.
- We do not accept cash or checks.
